Sahastradhara, Dehradun

The Thousandfold Spring – Nature, Waterfalls & Adventure

Overview

Sahastradhara, meaning “Thousandfold Spring,” is a scenic spot in Dehradun, famous for its natural sulphur springs with therapeutic benefits. The area features terraced waterfalls, small pools, and walking trails amidst lush greenery. Families, couples, and solo travelers can enjoy a full day exploring the serene surroundings, picnic areas, and photography spots.

Visitors can also explore nearby forested trails, seasonal blooms, and birdwatching areas. Monsoon brings a spectacular flow of waterfalls, while winter offers a peaceful retreat with cool air. Small local shops and food stalls make the visit more convenient and enjoyable.

Early mornings are ideal for yoga, meditation, or quiet walks, while evenings offer breathtaking sunset views. Photography enthusiasts will find plenty of opportunities to capture the misty hills, flowing springs, and vibrant natural scenery.

Highlights

  • Natural sulphur springs with therapeutic benefits
  • Terraced waterfalls and small dipping pools
  • Walking trails and viewpoints
  • Family-friendly picnic spots
  • Seasonal flora, birdwatching, and butterflies
  • Local food stalls and souvenir shops
