Dhanaulti Eco Park

A scenic eco-friendly park nestled in the Dhanaulti hills — perfect for nature walks, family outings, and photography.

Overview

Dhanaulti Eco Park is located amidst dense forests and rolling hills. The park is designed for eco-tourism with well-maintained walking trails, swings, viewpoints, and a picnic area. Families and nature lovers come to enjoy the tranquility and panoramic views of the Himalayas.

The park is well-kept with eco-friendly initiatives, including planting zones and nature awareness boards. Local vendors sell snacks, hot beverages, and small souvenirs. The terrain is easy to walk, suitable for all ages.

Visitors should carry water, wear comfortable shoes, and avoid littering. Early mornings or late afternoons provide the best lighting and cooler temperatures for walks and photography.

Highlights

  • Scenic walking trails & viewpoints
  • Family-friendly picnic areas & swings
  • Eco-friendly initiatives and nature awareness boards
  • Photography & bird-watching opportunities
  • Combine with Surkanda Devi or Sem Mukhem Temple
Quick tips:

Best visiting hours: 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Carry water and snacks. Respect nature, avoid plastics, and follow park guidelines. Early morning visits are less crowded.
