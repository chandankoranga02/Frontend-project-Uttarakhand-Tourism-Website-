Tehri Dam / Tehri Lake

Majestic reservoir & hydroelectric marvel, surrounded by lush Himalayan hills — perfect for boating & photography.

Overview

Tehri Dam is the tallest dam in India and a popular tourist attraction, forming the scenic Tehri Lake. Surrounded by hills and forests, the area offers boating, water sports, and riverside walks. The dam itself is an engineering marvel, drawing enthusiasts of architecture and hydroelectric projects.

The lakeside has several resorts and eateries. Photography enthusiasts often visit early morning or sunset for breathtaking reflections on the water. The serene environment makes it ideal for picnics, day trips, and short treks around the lake.

Visitors should check water levels and weather conditions, especially during monsoon, as some activities may be restricted. Local guides provide boat rentals and sightseeing advice.

Highlights

  • Tallest dam in India
  • Boating & water sports
  • Scenic sunrise & sunset photography
  • Nearby resorts & lakeside cafes
  • Combine with nearby Uttarakhand hill stations
Quick tips:

Best visited from Oct–Mar. Carry sunscreen & hats. Check boat availability in advance. Avoid peak monsoon due to high water levels.
