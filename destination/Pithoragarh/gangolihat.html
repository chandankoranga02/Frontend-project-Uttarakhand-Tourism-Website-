Historic temple dedicated to Goddess Kali, perched on a hill offering divine views and cultural insights.

Overview

Hatkalika Temple in Gangolihat is a revered shrine devoted to Goddess Kali. The temple, with its ancient stone architecture, sits atop a small hill and provides panoramic views of the surrounding valley. Devotees come here for religious rituals and to witness the sunset over the Himalayan backdrop.

The temple complex includes smaller shrines and sacred water tanks. Visitors enjoy serene walks and local markets near the entrance offering prasad and handicrafts.

Highlights

  • Ancient Kali temple with historical significance
  • Panoramic views of surrounding valleys and hills
  • Sunset photography opportunities
  • Local market and prasad stalls
  • Accessible hilltop walk
Visitor Tips:

Wear comfortable shoes for uphill walking. Respect temple rules. Early mornings and evenings are less crowded. Carry water and avoid littering.
