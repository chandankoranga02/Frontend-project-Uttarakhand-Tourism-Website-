Overview

Khaliya Bugyal is a high-altitude meadow located in Pithoragarh district, renowned for its vast green expanses and stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayan peaks. It’s a popular destination for trekkers and nature enthusiasts seeking tranquility amidst pristine landscapes.

The meadow is accessible via moderate trekking trails and is dotted with seasonal wildflowers during spring and early summer. Camping on the Bugyal provides an immersive experience in nature, with clear skies for stargazing.

Highlights

  • Vast alpine meadow with panoramic Himalayan views
  • Trekking and camping opportunities
  • Seasonal wildflowers and lush greenery
  • Birdwatching and photography
  • Peaceful natural environment, far from city crowds
Visitor Tips:

Wear trekking shoes and carry warm clothes. Start early for best views. Respect nature and carry back all waste. Avoid camping during heavy rains or snow.

Quick Facts

Best Season: Mar–Jun & Sep–Nov

Stay Duration: 1–2 days

Difficulty: Moderate trekking

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Pithoragarh (~70 km)

Road: Base village accessible via road; trek of ~5–6 km to Bugyal

Nearest Railhead: Kathgodam (~280 km)

Facilities

  • Local guides for trekking
  • Camping spots (with prior permission)
  • Minimal eateries in nearby villages
