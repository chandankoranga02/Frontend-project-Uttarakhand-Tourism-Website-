Overview

Pantnagar University Campus, officially G.B. Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, offers beautifully landscaped gardens and tree-lined avenues. The botanical walk provides visitors a chance to explore a variety of plants, trees, and ornamental species.

The campus is peaceful, ideal for nature enthusiasts, students, and photographers. It also hosts educational tours focusing on botany and ecology, and provides shaded benches for relaxation along the walking trails.

Highlights

  • Botanical walkways and themed gardens
  • Educational and leisure exploration
  • Photography of trees, flowers, and landscaped areas
  • Peaceful, family-friendly environment
  • Proximity to Pantnagar town and transport
Visitor Tips:

Carry water and sun protection. Walk leisurely and respect plantings. Photography is encouraged but avoid disturbing plant life.
