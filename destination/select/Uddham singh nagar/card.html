Uddham singh nagar Sites

Amritpur Area Cafes

Amritpur area, along the Haldwani–Nainital road, hosts several cozy cafes ideal for travelers seeking refreshment and relaxation.

Gularbhoj Dam

Gularbhoj Dam is a calm water reservoir surrounded by forested hills, perfect for picnics and short nature trips. The dam area offers walking trails, viewpoints, and a peaceful atmosphere away from urban life.

Jim Corbett National Park

Jim Corbett National Park, located in Ramnagar, Uttarakhand, is a wildlife lover’s paradise. Established in 1936, it is India’s first national park and a renowned tiger reserve. Visitors can enjoy jeep safaris, birdwatching, and nature walks.

Pantnagar University Campus

Pantnagar University Campus, officially G.B. Pant University of Agriculture & Technology, offers beautifully landscaped gardens and tree-lined avenues.

Rudrapur Lake

Rudrapur Lake is a serene waterbody located in the heart of Rudrapur city. The calm waters and surrounding walking paths make it ideal for families, couples, and solo visitors looking to relax.
