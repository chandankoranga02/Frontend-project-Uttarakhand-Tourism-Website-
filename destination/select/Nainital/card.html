Nainital Sites

Bhimtal

Bhimtal is centered around a picturesque freshwater lake with a small island at its heart. Compared to Naini Lake, Bhimtal is calmer and better suited for relaxed stays,

Eco Cave Gardens

Eco Cave Gardens is a compact park made of interlinked rock caves, terraced gardens, and themed enclosures located near the Mall Road in Nainital.

Ramgarh

Nestled at an altitude of around 1789 meters in Nainital district, Ramgarh is known as the “Fruit Bowl of Kumaon” due to its rich orchards of apples, plums, peaches, and apricots.

Kaichi Dham

Kaichi Dham, also known as the Neem Karoli Baba Ashram, is a famous spiritual retreat located about 17 km from Nainital, on the Nainital–Almora road.

Naini Lake

Naini Lake is a picturesque natural freshwater body surrounded by the bustling town of Nainital and hills.
