Uttarkashi Sites

Gangotri Temple

Gangotri Temple is a sacred shrine in the Himalayas, marking the origin of the holy Ganges River. Pilgrims visit to offer prayers to Goddess Ganga and perform rituals along the riverbank.

Gangnani Hot Water Spring

Gangnani Hot Water Spring is nestled on the banks of the Bhagirathi River. Locals and trekkers frequent this natural spring for its therapeutic warm waters.

Harsil Valley

A serene Himalayan valley on the banks of Bhagirathi River, surrounded by pine forests and snow-capped peaks, ideal for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Maneri Dam

Maneri Dam is a picturesque site built on the Bhagirathi River. It is primarily used for hydroelectricity generation and water storage but has also become a peaceful spot for tourists and photographers.

Yamunotri Temple

Yamunotri Temple is a revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Yamuna. Situated amidst high mountains and hot water springs, the temple sees devotees undertaking a challenging trek or pony ride from Janki Chatti.
