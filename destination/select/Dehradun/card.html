Dehradun Sites

Forest Research Institute

Historic colonial-era campus, botanical museum, long shaded avenues and photogenic arches — perfect for history, nature and architecture lovers.

Maldevta Wetland

Maldevta is a small but ecologically important wetland located a short drive north-east of central Dehradun.

Robber’s Cave

Robber’s Cave, locally called Guchhupani, is a narrow, river-carved gorge tucked into the hills near Dehradun city

Sahastradhara

Sahastradhara, meaning “Thousandfold Spring,” is a scenic spot in Dehradun, famous for its natural sulphur springs with therapeutic benefits

Tiger Falls

Tiger Falls is one of the most impressive waterfalls near Dehradun, located in the Chakrata region.

Tapkeshwar Temple

Tapkeshwar Mahadev sits inside a natural limestone cave alongside a small stream. The unique draw is the continuous dripping of groundwater onto the Shivalinga
