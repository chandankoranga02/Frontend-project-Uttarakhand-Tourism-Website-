Almora Sites

Binsar Wildlife Sanctuary

Dense oak forests, Himalayan peaks, and over 200 bird species — Binsar is one of Kumaon’s most serene and scenic wildlife sanctuaries.

Chitai Temple

Famous hill-temple dedicated to Golu Devta — known for the bell-covered shrine and thousands of legal-style wish petitions.

Deer Park

Peaceful forest zone near Almora — home to deer, birds, and serene walking trails amidst pine and oak forests.

Jageshwar Temple

Cluster of ancient stone temples set in deodar forest — one of Kumaon's finest temple groups with archaeological and spiritual value.

Lakhudiyar Cave

Rock art gallery on limestone shelters — twin sites with prehistoric petroglyphs and cultural inscriptions near Almora.
