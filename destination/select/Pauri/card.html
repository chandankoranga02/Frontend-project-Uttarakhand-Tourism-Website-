Pauri Sites

Kandoliya Temple

Kandoliya Temple is perched on a hilltop surrounded by dense pine and oak forests. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is a peaceful retreat away from the town bustle.

Pauri Town Sunset Point

Pauri Town Sunset Point offers unobstructed views of the valley and surrounding Himalayan foothills. Visitors gather in the evening to watch the golden hour and capture stunning panoramic photographs.

Satpuli Riverside

Satpuli Riverside is located along the scenic banks of the Nayar River. The area is known for its tranquil environment, shallow swimming spots, and smooth rock beds.

Tarkeshwar Mahadev Temple

Tarkeshwar Mahadev Temple is located about 9 km from Pauri, perched atop a hill surrounded by dense oak and pine forests.

Adwani Village Trek

The Adwani Village trek is a moderate hike through pine and oak forests, terraced fields, and small streams.
