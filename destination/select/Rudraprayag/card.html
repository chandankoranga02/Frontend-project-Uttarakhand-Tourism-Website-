Rudraprayag Sites

Chopta (Mini Switzerland)

hopta is a small region in Uttarakhand, part of the Kedarnath wildlife sanctuary. Its lush green meadows and scenic backdrop of Himalayan peaks

Dhari Devi Temple

Dhari Devi Temple is considered a powerful spiritual site in Uttarakhand. Situated on the banks of the Alaknanda River, it is revered for its ancient idol and religious significance.

Kedarnath Temple

Kedarnath Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is part of the Char Dham pilgrimage circuit. Located at an altitude of 3,583 meters, it is accessible via a trek of ~16 km from Gaurikund or by helicopter services from Phata or Guptkashi.

Koteshwar Mahadev Cave

CKoteshwar Mahadev Cave is a sacred cave shrine situated near Rudraprayag. It is renowned for its natural rock formation that houses a Shiva Linga inside the cave

Ukhimath

Ukhimath is a small town in Rudraprayag district, known for its winter shrine of Kedarnath temple deity. Devotees visit the town during winter months when the Kedarnath shrine is closed due to snow.
