Bageshwar Sites

Bagnath temple

Historic Shiva temple standing at the confluence of the Gomati and Saryu rivers — spiritual heart of Bageshwar town.

Gauri Udiyar Cave Temple

Ancient rock-cut temple dedicated to Goddess Gauri, surrounded by scenic hills and serene forests.

Pindari Glacier Trek

Classic Himalayan glacier trek in Kumaon — scenic moraines, high-altitude camps, and dramatic views of Nanda Devi and other peaks.

Sunderdhunga Trek

Sunderdhunga Trek is one of Uttarakhand's most scenic trails, offering breathtaking views of towering Himalayan peaks, verdant valleys, and alpine meadows.

Vijaypur Viewpoint

Vijaypur Viewpoint is a tranquil hilltop spot in Uttarakhand offering breathtaking views of surrounding Himalayan ranges
