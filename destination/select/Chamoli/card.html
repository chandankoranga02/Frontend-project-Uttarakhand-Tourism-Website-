Chamoli Sites

Badrinath Temple

Badrinath Temple, one of the holiest Hindu shrines, stands on the banks of the Alaknanda River in Chamoli district.

Valley of Flowers National Park

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — a paradise of alpine blossoms, snow peaks, and peaceful serenity in Chamoli district.

Joshimath

The gateway to several Himalayan expeditions — blending spiritual depth and alpine beauty in Uttarakhand’s Chamoli district.

Hemkund Sahib

Hemkund Sahib, also known as Gurudwara Sri Hemkunt Sahib Ji, lies in the Chamoli district of Uttarakhand at an altitude of 4,632 meters.

Nanda Devi National Park Region

A UNESCO World Heritage Site — a sanctuary of untouched Himalayan wilderness, rare flora, and spectacular mountain vistas.
