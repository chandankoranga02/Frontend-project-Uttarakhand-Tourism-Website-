Haridwar Sites

Rajaji National Park

Vast sal & mixed-forest park with rich wildlife (elephants, leopards, deer) — ideal for jeep safaris and birding in the Haridwar–Chilla sectors.

Har Ki Pauri

Har Ki Pauri is the most revered ghat in Haridwar and hosts the daily evening Ganga Aarti which draws thousands of pilgrims.

Chandi Devi Temple

Chandi Devi Temple sits on Neel Parvat and is accessible by a ropeway (cable car) or by a walking trail.

Daksha Mahadev Temple

Daksha Mahadev Temple at Kankhal is associated with the legendary Daksha Yajna and Sati myth

Bilkeshwar Mahadev Temple

Bilkeshwar Mahadev sits in the valley of Bilva Parvat close to Haridwar railway station and Har Ki Pauri.

Bharat Mata Mandir

Bharat Mata Mandir in Haridwar is a multi-storey temple dedicated to Mother India with a series of floors representing maps, statues and cultural exhibits.
