Pithoragarh Sites

Askot Wildlife Sanctuary

Askot Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area in Pithoragarh district, spread across dense forests, alpine meadows, and river valleys.

Pithoragarh Fort

Pithoragarh Fort is a historic structure perched atop a hill overlooking the Pithoragarh valley. Constructed centuries ago, it has witnessed multiple rulers

Gangolihat

Hatkalika Temple in Gangolihat is a revered shrine devoted to Goddess Kali. The temple, with its ancient stone architecture, sits atop a small hill and provides panoramic views of the surrounding valley.

Khaliya Bugyal

Khaliya Bugyal is a high-altitude meadow located in Pithoragarh district, renowned for its vast green expanses and stunning views of the snow-capped Himalayan peaks.

Munsiyari

Munsiyari is a small hill station in Pithoragarh district, famous for panoramic views of the Himalayan range, including Panchachuli peaks.
