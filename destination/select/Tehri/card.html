Tehri Sites

Dhanaulti Eco Park

Dhanaulti Eco Park is located amidst dense forests and rolling hills. The park is designed for eco-tourism with well-maintained walking trails, swings, viewpoints, and a picnic area

Sem Mukhem Temple

Sem Mukhem Temple is a lesser-known temple near Dhanaulti, nestled in quiet pine forests. Its peaceful ambiance makes it ideal for meditation, photography, and a short nature retreat.

Devprayag

Devprayag is where the Alaknanda and Bhagirathi rivers merge to form the holy Ganga. The town is dotted with temples, ghats, and narrow lanes, giving visitors a spiritual and cultural experience.

Surkanda Devi Temple

Surkanda Devi Temple sits atop a hill near Dhanaulti and is known for its scenic trekking route and spiritual significance.

Tehri Dam

Tehri Dam is the tallest dam in India and a popular tourist attraction, forming the scenic Tehri Lake. Surrounded by hills and forests, the area offers boating, water sports, and riverside walks.
