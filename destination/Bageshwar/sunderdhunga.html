Overview

Sunderdhunga Trek is one of Uttarakhand's most scenic trails, offering breathtaking views of towering Himalayan peaks, verdant valleys, and alpine meadows. It’s ideal for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers who enjoy trekking off the beaten path.

The trek begins at a base village and follows river streams, waterfalls, and dense pine forests. Small campsites are available along the way for overnight stays. During summers, the route is decorated with wildflowers, while winters offer pristine snow-covered landscapes.

Highlights

  • Panoramic Himalayan views and sunrise spots
  • Alpine meadows and diverse flora
  • River streams, waterfalls, and natural pools
  • Moderate trekking route suitable for beginners to intermediate trekkers
  • Camping and photography opportunities
Tips for Trekkers:

Carry trekking shoes, water, warm clothing, and a small first-aid kit. Start early to avoid afternoon rains. Hire a local guide for navigation and safety. Leave no litter behind and respect local wildlife.

Quick Facts

Best Season: May–Oct

Trek Duration: 2–4 days depending on pace

Difficulty: Moderate

How to Reach

Nearest Town: Dharchula (~45 km)

Road: Accessible by local transport to base village

Nearest Railhead: Kathgodam (~250 km)

Facilities

  • Basic camping facilities along the route
  • Local homestays at base villages
  • Guides and porters available for hire
