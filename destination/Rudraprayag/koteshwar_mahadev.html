Koteshwar Mahadev Cave

Hidden in the hills, this cave temple dedicated to Lord Shiva offers a tranquil setting with natural rock formations and a scenic riverside.

Overview

Koteshwar Mahadev Cave is a sacred cave shrine situated near Rudraprayag. It is renowned for its natural rock formation that houses a Shiva Linga inside the cave, with water dripping from the stalactites.

The cave is small but atmospheric, attracting both pilgrims and nature lovers. A small trek through a forested path leads to the cave entrance, offering a serene experience away from crowded temples.

Nearby, the Alaknanda river flows, adding charm to the location. Basic facilities are present, including local food stalls at the parking area, but visitors should carry water and snacks if trekking in.

Highlights

  • Ancient cave shrine with Shiva Linga
  • Beautiful river and forest surroundings
  • Short trek adds adventure
  • Quiet, less-crowded spiritual spot
  • Photogenic natural formations inside the cave
Quick Tips:

Best visited in summer or post-monsoon. Trekking shoes recommended. Respect temple customs. Avoid plastic inside the cave.
