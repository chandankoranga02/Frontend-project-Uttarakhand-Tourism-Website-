Overview

Lohaghat is a charming town known for its scenic beauty and pleasant climate. Located near the Saryu River, it offers peaceful walks through lush green hills, terraced farms, and local markets.

The town has historical significance with colonial-era structures and proximity to ancient temples. It’s ideal for nature lovers, photographers, and those seeking a quiet getaway from city life.

Highlights

  • Scenic green hills and terraced farms
  • Peaceful riverside walks
  • Local temples and historical sites
  • Photography and birdwatching
  • Quiet, offbeat destination
Visitor Tips:

Carry comfortable shoes for hill walks. Best to visit between Mar–Jun and Sep–Nov. Respect local culture and avoid littering.
