Overview

Purnagiri Temple is a revered shrine situated on a hill in Champawat district. Devotees climb a series of stone steps to reach the temple, which provides breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and peaks.

The temple is known for its serene ambiance, and attracts pilgrims during festivals, particularly Navratri. Local legends describe the goddess as fulfilling wishes and providing protection.

Highlights

  • Hilltop temple with panoramic views
  • Spiritual pilgrimage site
  • Festivals during Navratri attract thousands
  • Stone steps and scenic hill paths
  • Photography of sunrise and surrounding peaks
Visitor Tips:

Wear comfortable shoes for climbing steps. Visit early morning for peace and clear views. Carry water and respect temple customs.
