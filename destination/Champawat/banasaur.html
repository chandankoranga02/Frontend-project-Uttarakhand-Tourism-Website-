Banasur Fort

Legendary fort atop a hill, surrounded by dense forests and mythological tales of demon king Banasur.

Overview

Banasur Fort is a historic fort located on a hill surrounded by lush forests. According to local legend, it was built by the demon king Banasur. The fort offers a mix of history, folklore, and trekking opportunities.

Visitors can explore the fort ruins, enjoy views of the surrounding hills and valleys, and connect with nature while walking through forest trails. It's a quieter destination ideal for history enthusiasts and trekkers.

Highlights

  • Historic fort with folklore significance
  • Hilltop trekking and panoramic views
  • Forest surroundings with nature trails
  • Photography of ruins and landscapes
  • Peaceful, offbeat destination
Visitor Tips:

Wear sturdy shoes for trekking. Carry water, snacks, and sun protection. Best visited between Mar–Jun and Sep–Nov. Respect forest and ruins.
