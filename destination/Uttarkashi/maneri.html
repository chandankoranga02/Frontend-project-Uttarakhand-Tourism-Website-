Maneri Dam

A scenic dam on the Bhagirathi River near Uttarkashi, providing hydroelectric power, water storage, and beautiful riverine vistas.

Overview

Maneri Dam is a picturesque site built on the Bhagirathi River. It is primarily used for hydroelectricity generation and water storage but has also become a peaceful spot for tourists and photographers. The surrounding landscape offers lush green valleys and clear river waters.

Visitors can enjoy short walks along the dam, birdwatching, and capturing the pristine Himalayan backdrop. The site is accessible by road from Uttarkashi and is often included in tours covering Gangotri and Harsil.

Facilities are basic, with a few small eateries nearby and limited accommodation options in the surrounding villages.

Highlights

  • Hydroelectric dam on Bhagirathi River
  • Scenic Himalayan valley views
  • Peaceful environment for photography
  • Birdwatching opportunities
  • Accessible by road from Uttarkashi
Travel Tips:

Visit from April to October for pleasant weather. Carry water and snacks, and wear comfortable shoes. Limited facilities on site, so plan accordingly.
