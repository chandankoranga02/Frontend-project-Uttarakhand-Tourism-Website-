Gangnani Hot Water Spring

A natural hot water spring in Uttarkashi, believed to have medicinal properties and a perfect stop for relaxation during Himalayan treks.

Overview

Gangnani Hot Water Spring is nestled on the banks of the Bhagirathi River. Locals and trekkers frequent this natural spring for its therapeutic warm waters. The surrounding area has beautiful Himalayan views, pine forests, and walking trails.

The site is small, tranquil, and less crowded than other tourist spots, making it ideal for short stops during trips to Harsil, Yamunotri, or Gangotri. People often soak their feet or take a short bath, believing in the spring’s medicinal qualities.

Nearby villages offer simple accommodation and eateries, giving a rustic Himalayan experience to visitors.

Highlights

  • Natural hot water spring with therapeutic benefits
  • Scenic Himalayan surroundings
  • Short walk along Bhagirathi River
  • Peaceful and less crowded
  • Nearby trekking routes to Harsil and Yamunotri
Travel Tips:

Best visited from April to October. Wear shoes for rocky paths. Carry a small towel and avoid leaving trash. Early morning visits are peaceful.
