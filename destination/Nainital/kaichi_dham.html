Overview

Kaichi Dham, also known as the Neem Karoli Baba Ashram, is a famous spiritual retreat located about 17 km from Nainital, on the Nainital–Almora road. The temple complex was established by the revered saint Neem Karoli Baba and attracts thousands of devotees and seekers from across the world, including global figures and tech leaders.

The temple is known for its serene ambiance, spiritual energy, and cleanliness. It is situated amidst lush green hills and near the Kosi River, creating a calm and meditative atmosphere. The ashram also runs charitable and community services for visitors and locals.

Kaichi Dham gained international recognition for its connection with followers such as Steve Jobs and Mark Zuckerberg, who visited the ashram during their early years.

Highlights

  • Temple and ashram of Neem Karoli Baba
  • Annual Bhandara and spiritual gatherings in June
  • Beautiful hill surroundings with peaceful river view
  • Famous among global spiritual seekers
  • Clean, well-maintained, and calm environment
Quick tips:

Footwear, phones, and photography are not allowed inside temple premises. Maintain silence and decorum. The best time to visit is morning hours before the crowd builds up.
