Bhimtal

Tranquil lake town near Nainital — larger, quieter lake with an island, boating, aquarium and scenic walks.

Overview

Bhimtal is centered around a picturesque freshwater lake with a small island at its heart. Compared to Naini Lake, Bhimtal is calmer and better suited for relaxed stays, long walks and birdwatching. The town has well-kept promenades, boating services, and a small aquarium near the lakeside.

The lake is ringed by hotels, cafes and gardens. Visitors can hire paddle boats, rowboats or take a lakeside stroll at sunset. The island can be visited (check local timings) and provides a peaceful spot away from the shoreline bustle.

Bhimtal works well as a quiet base for exploring nearby lakes (Naukuchiatal, Sattal), or as a one–two night stop for couples and families seeking calm.

Highlights

  • Large, serene lake with an island
  • Boating — paddle, row and shikaras
  • Small aquarium & park near the lake
  • Quiet promenades and sunset viewpoints
  • Close to Naukuchiatal and Sattal for day trips
Quick tips:

Early morning and late afternoon are best for calm water and birds. Check boat and island timings locally. Carry sunscreen and a light jacket for evenings.

Quick Facts

Avg time: 2–4 hours (or overnight)

Best season: Mar–Jun, Sep–Nov

Entry: Public access; boating fees apply

How to Reach

Nearest Airport: Pantnagar (~70 km)

Nearest Railhead: Kathgodam (~35 km)

Road: Frequent taxis/buses from Kathgodam/Nainital; driveable via well-maintained roads.

Stay & Food

  • Lakefront hotels and guesthouses
  • Cafes and family restaurants on the promenade
  • Local bakeries and street snacks
