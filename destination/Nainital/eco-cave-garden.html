Eco Cave Gardens

Fun family attraction in Nainital — a series of beautifully landscaped cave-like rock gardens, animal enclosures and kid-friendly trails.

Overview

Eco Cave Gardens is a compact park made of interlinked rock caves, terraced gardens, and themed enclosures located near the Mall Road in Nainital. It’s designed for a quick, entertaining visit — especially good for families with children and casual nature lovers.

The site includes illuminated caves, narrow walkways, small animal exhibits (local species/aviary), viewpoints overlooking the valley, and landscaped lawns. It’s short, accessible, and works well as a 45–90 minute stop while exploring Nainital town.

Facilities include ticket booth, a few snack stalls nearby, clean pathways and benches. Wear comfortable shoes (some cave passages are narrow and slightly slippery when wet).

Highlights

  • Interconnected artificial caves and rock gardens
  • Small animal/aviary enclosures and interpretive panels
  • Great for kids — short, safe routes and play areas
  • Close to Mall Road and Naini Lake — easy to combine
  • Good quick stop for photography and relaxing
Quick tips:

Best visited early morning or late afternoon to avoid crowds. Not suitable for large wheelchairs (narrow passages). Carry a small bottle of water. Avoid visiting during heavy rain — paths can be slippery.

Quick Facts

Avg time: 45–90 minutes

Timings: 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM (seasonal)

Entry: Paid — small fee

How to Reach

From Kathgodam: ~35 km — taxi or bus to Nainital, then short walk to Eco Cave Gardens.

Parking: Limited near Mall Road; use public parking and walk.

Facilities

  • Benches & shaded spots
  • Small snack stalls nearby
  • Guided signage and explanatory panels
