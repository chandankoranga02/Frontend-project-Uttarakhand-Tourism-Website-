Pauri Town Sunset Point

Experience breathtaking sunsets over the Pauri valley — a short evening outing with panoramic views.

Overview

Pauri Town Sunset Point offers unobstructed views of the valley and surrounding Himalayan foothills. Visitors gather in the evening to watch the golden hour and capture stunning panoramic photographs. The location is easily accessible from the town center, making it ideal for families, couples, and solo travelers.

The area is lined with trees and small benches, creating a calm, relaxed environment. Local tea stalls and snack vendors provide refreshments while visitors wait for the sunset. The site is safe and maintained, with minimal walking required to reach the viewpoint.

Sunset timings vary by season, and mornings offer misty valley views for photography enthusiasts. The point can also be combined with other short excursions such as Satpuli Riverside and Kandoliya Temple.

Highlights

  • Panoramic valley and Himalayan foothill views
  • Perfect spot for sunset photography
  • Easy access — short walk from town
  • Local tea stalls and snack vendors nearby
  • Combine with Satpuli Riverside or Kandoliya Temple
Quick tips:

Arrive 30–40 minutes before sunset for best views. Carry a camera or smartphone tripod. Respect local vendors and avoid littering.
