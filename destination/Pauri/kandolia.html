Kandoliya Temple

Ancient hilltop temple offering panoramic views and spiritual serenity in Pauri Garhwal.

Overview

Kandoliya Temple is perched on a hilltop surrounded by dense pine and oak forests. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is a peaceful retreat away from the town bustle. Pilgrims visit for daily worship, and visitors come to enjoy panoramic views of Pauri valley and distant Himalayan peaks.

The temple architecture is traditional, with simple stone and wooden carvings. During festivals, the temple sees an influx of devotees, creating a vibrant spiritual atmosphere. Paths leading to the temple are well-maintained and suitable for short treks and photography walks.

Local vendors offer prasad, chai, and small handicrafts. Early morning visits offer misty views, while sunset provides breathtaking golden light over the valley.

Highlights

  • Hilltop Shiva temple with serene surroundings
  • Panoramic views of Pauri valley and Himalayas
  • Short trek and photography opportunities
  • Local vendors offering snacks and prasad
  • Festivals attract colorful cultural experience
Quick tips:

Wear comfortable trekking shoes. Early mornings or late afternoons are best for photography. Respect local customs and avoid littering.
