Overview

Satpuli Riverside is located along the scenic banks of the Nayar River. The area is known for its tranquil environment, shallow swimming spots, and smooth rock beds. Families and travelers visit to relax, picnic, and enjoy short nature walks along the riverside.

The riverside is dotted with local tea stalls, small eateries, and vendors selling handicrafts. The calm waters are perfect for children to play and for photography enthusiasts to capture reflections of the hills in the river. The surrounding forested hills add a natural charm to this getaway.

Visitors should carry water, snacks, and swimwear if planning to take a dip. Early morning or late afternoon provides the best lighting for photography, and the area is quieter compared to central Pauri.

Highlights

  • Scenic riverside with calm waters
  • Family-friendly picnic spots & shallow swimming
  • Short walks along forested hills
  • Photography & nature exploration
  • Combine with Pauri sunset point or Kandoliya Temple
Quick tips:

Bring water, snacks, and swimming essentials. Avoid deep river areas. Best visited during dry season (Oct–May) for safe water levels.
