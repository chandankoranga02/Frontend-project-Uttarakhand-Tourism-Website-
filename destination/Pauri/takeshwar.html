Tarkeshwar Mahadev Temple

A serene hilltop temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, surrounded by lush forests and scenic views.

Overview

Tarkeshwar Mahadev Temple is located about 9 km from Pauri, perched atop a hill surrounded by dense oak and pine forests. The temple is famous for its peaceful ambiance, the gentle tinkling of bells, and panoramic views of the surrounding valleys.

Visitors often trek up from the base of the hill, enjoying forest trails, fresh mountain air, and occasional wildlife sightings. The temple attracts both devotees and nature lovers alike, providing a perfect combination of spirituality and adventure.

Facilities are basic, with small shops selling prasad and tea. The best time to visit is early morning or late afternoon, especially during the monsoon when the forest becomes lush and vibrant.

Highlights

  • Hilltop Shiva temple with tranquil surroundings
  • Moderate trek through dense oak and pine forests
  • Panoramic views of Pauri valley
  • Photography & meditation spot
  • Combine with nearby Kandoliya Temple or Pauri town for day trips
Quick tips:

Wear comfortable shoes for trekking, carry water and light snacks. Avoid visiting during heavy rains due to slippery trails.
